Sigtuna Swedens Oldest City

This website can be used as your personal guide through Sigtuna. Find your point of interest and take it from there!

The city is located slightly northwest of the capital Stockholm and has had a big impact on the Swedens import and export

Sigtuna used to be a big trading port with the goal out in the open world. After that it became the seat of the first christian king Olof Skötkonung.The city has been marked by his presence and made a big impact on the rest of the country as well.
